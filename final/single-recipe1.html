Carne Asada

Dig into these big, beefy bakes. They're the hearty, comforting weeknight classics the family craves.

prep time

25 min

Cook time

40 min

Servings

4 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast Carrots food

Instructions

Step-1

Place the meat directly over the hot half of the grill and leave the lid open.

Step-2

Cook the meat, flipping it often, until it is beautifully charred on both sides, about 6-8 minutes total cooking time. The center of the meat should register 128-130°F on an instant-read meat thermometer.

Step-3

Transfer the steaks to a cutting board and let them rest 5 minutes (this will allow the juices to redistribute). Slice the meat against the grain. Drizzle with lime juice. Serve with salsa, sliced avocado, sliced onion, chopped cilantro, crumbled Queso Fresco cheese and tortillas. Enjoy!

Ingredients

1 lb. beef skirt steak 450g, can also use flank steak, top sirloin also fine

1 lime juice ie juice from 1 lime

¼ cup orange juice

¼ teaspoon cumin

¼ teaspoon salt

¼ teaspoon pepper

2 cloves garlic large, 3 if smaller
