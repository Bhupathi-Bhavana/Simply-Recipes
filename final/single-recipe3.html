vegetable soup

A Vegetable Soup recipe is so healthy and there are so many ways you can make them. Get cooking two easy classic nutritious recipes of vegetable soup loaded with mixed veggies and high on flavors.

prep time

30 min

Cook time

15 min

Servings

4 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast Carrots food

Instructions

Step-1

Sweat onions, carrots, and celery in olive oil and tomato paste. The tomato paste adds lots of flavor to the soup.

Step-2

Add more flavor with garlic, ground fennel seeds (or use other spices), pepper, and salt. Pour in vegetable or chicken stock, a can of diced tomatoes, potatoes, cabbage, and bay leaves.

Step-3

Simmer until the veggies are tender (about 20 minutes). Add frozen peas and cook for five more minutes.

Ingredients

3 tablespoons olive oil

2 cups chopped onion (1 medium)

2 cups chopped potato (2 medium)

6 cups stock or broth, see our ultra-satisfying veggie broth or homemade chicken stock

¼ teaspoon salt

1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes with their liquid

1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt, plus more to taste

2 tablespoons tomato paste

1 ½ cups chopped carrot (3 to 4 carrots)

4 teaspoons minced garlic (4 cloves)

3/4 teaspoon ground fennel seed

1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

1 cup frozen peas

1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar or fresh lemon juice, optional
